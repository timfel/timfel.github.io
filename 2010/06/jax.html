Git talk @ JaX 2010

by Tim Felgentreff , 2010/06/07, 00:00 UTC

I had the opportunity to speak at the JaX 2010 in Mainz, Germany, together with Ralph Guderlei from exxcellent solutions. The JaX is one of the largest Java conferences and primarily aimed at enterprise architectures and SOA. Our talk concerned distributed version control systems, how they compare to CVS-style systems and how to integrate them into existing development environments with a special focus on Git. The slides are available in German.
